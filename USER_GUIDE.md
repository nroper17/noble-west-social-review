# Noble West Social Review Tool — User Instructions

**Live Dashboard:** [https://noble-west-social-review-e1b7.vercel.app](https://noble-west-social-review-e1b7.vercel.app)

Welcome to the Noble West Social Review Tool! This platform is designed to seamlessly manage social media calendars and collaborate directly with clients, keeping internal discussions private while eliminating the need for bulky spreadsheets.

Here is exactly how to manage your daily workflow:

## 1. Getting Started: Workspaces

A **Workspace** represents a single client, brand, or partner. Only **NW Admins** can create Workspaces.
- Log in using your Google account.
- Click **Admin** in the top navigation bar.
- Create a new Workspace (e.g., "Nike Spring Campaign").
- **Assign Team Members:** You must check the boxes next to the coworkers who should have access to this Workspace. If they are not assigned to it, they cannot see it.

## 2. Managing the Calendar

Once inside a Workspace, you'll see a monthly layout.
- Click any empty date square to create a new Draft Post.
- **Dates & Platforms:** Select the date the post should go live, and check the social platform icons (Instagram, LinkedIn, etc.) — these dictate the colored badges that display on the calendar grid.
- **Assets:** Upload images or GIFs directly into the carousel. If you have video (MP4s), paste the Dropbox link and the tool will automatically convert it into an inline video player!
- **Status:** Slide the status from `Draft` to `Pending Review`. This dictates the color of the post on the calendar grid.

## 3. Collaboration & Comments

The right-hand panel features our split-commenting system to protect internal workflows:

**🔒 Internal Thread (Team Only)**
Anything typed in the `Internal Thread` tab is mathematically invisible to the client. Use this to discuss strategy, request asset revisions from coworkers, and finalize copy before pushing the post to `Pending Review`.
- Type `@` to pull up a floating autocomplete menu of your teammates' emails. 
- You can heavily customize threaded replies underneath each other.
- When an objective is met, click the `Resolve` checkmark to gray the comment out for the team, or click the `Trash` button to delete it entirely from history.

**💬 Client Thread (Shared View)**
Comments left here are completely visible when the client views the Magic Link. If a client relies, it shows up here.

## 4. Client Review

We designed the Client Portal to be completely frictionless. Clients **never** have to create an account, memorize passwords, or sign up. 
- Click on **Workspace Settings** in the top navigation.
- Locate the **Magic Link**. 
- Copy this URL and email it to your client! 
- When they click it, it securely logs them into a read-only portal where they can view the calendar, leave feedback in the Client Thread, and change Post Statuses to `Approved` or `Needs Revision`.

*Security Note:* If the client accidentally leaks the link to someone outside their company, go to Workspace Settings and click **Regenerate Link**. The old link will instantly break forever.

## 5. Exporting PDF Presentations
If you need to send a high-fidelity snapshot of the campaign for a legal review or leadership deck:
- Click the **Export PDF** button at the top of the Workspace calendar.
- The tool automatically generates an 8.5x11 PDF document. 
- The PDF perfectly formats every post, image thumbnail, and client comment into a readable document, **while strictly excluding** all internal communication and notes.
